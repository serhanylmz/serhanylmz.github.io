I am a senior-year undergraduate student in Computer Science at Sabanci University, with a cGPA of 3.63/4. Currently, I'm working as a Research Assistant, working with Prof. Kemal Oflazer focusing on precise hallucination detection.

I'm also collaborating with Prof. Iryna Gurevych and Anmol Goel at TU Darmstadt, developing reliable AI agents and inspecting agentic behavior.

I am a contributor to Project Aya by Cohere for AI to accelerate multilingual and multimodal AI.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at KTH Royal Institute of Technology under Prof. Gabriel Skantze, developing advanced AI agents for turn-taking conversation. I also spent a summer as an intern at EPFL Lausanne, where I initiated Project Charisius for developing robust, privacy-preserving Federated ML environments.

At Sabanci University, I founded and lead kAi Sabanci, the university's premier AI club, growing membership to over 600 students. My leadership and achievements in AI have been recognized by the NVIDIA Student Spotlight series.

Research Interests

AI Agents, Conversational AI, Systems for Retrieval and Reasoning and many more. I'm really excited to work towards human-like multimodal interactions in large language models.
